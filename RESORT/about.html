About Us

Experience Unforgettable Moments at La Vanessa Beach Resort

Welcome to La Vanessa Beach Resort! At La Vanessa Beach Resort, we believe in the power of relaxation, rejuvenation, and creating unforgettable experiences. Nestled along the pristine shores of a tropical paradise, our resort offers a haven of serenity and natural beauty that will captivate your senses. Our resort is designed to provide the perfect balance between modern luxury and the charm of the coastal environment. With a range of accommodations to suit every traveler's needs, from cozy rooms to spacious cottages, we ensure that your stay is comfortable, inviting, and tailored to your preferences. Immerse yourself in the breathtaking beauty of our beachfront location. Feel the soft sand between your toes as you stroll along the shoreline, listen to the soothing sound of waves crashing against the shore, and soak up the warm tropical sun as you relax by the pool. Indulge your taste buds with a delightful culinary experience at our on-site restaurant, where our talented chefs prepare a delectable array of dishes using locally sourced ingredients. From fresh seafood to international cuisine, our menu is crafted to satisfy even the most discerning palate. At La Vanessa Beach Resort, we understand the importance of creating unforgettable memories. Whether you're seeking adventure or tranquility, our resort offers a range of activities to cater to every interest. Explore the vibrant underwater world with snorkeling or diving, embark on island-hopping tours to nearby gems, or simply unwind with a soothing massage at our spa. We take pride in our warm and attentive staff, who are dedicated to providing exceptional service and ensuring that your every need is met. From the moment you arrive until the time of your departure, we strive to exceed your expectations and make your stay a truly memorable one. Come and experience the magic of La Vanessa Beach Resort, where breathtaking natural beauty meets unparalleled hospitality. Let us be your gateway to an unforgettable beachside getaway, where relaxation, adventure, and cherished moments await.

Our Mission, Vision and Core Values

The Group’s growth philosophy is clearly articulated in our Vision, Mission, and Core Values. Based upon these Values is where and why we are consistently and wholly committed to sustain a stabilizing balance among such considerations as optimal guest experiences, profitability, human capital, as well as in respecting the environment around us.

Vision

We aspire to be acknowledged regionally and accepted globally as one of the most efficiently managed hospitality groups wherein our staff at both managerial and service levels consistently strive to deliver the highest of service standards at all times and under all circumstances.

Mission

We are unswervingly committed in providing and delivering outstanding services and experiences to new and returning guests by exceeding their expectations of what we can offer, by recognizing and acknowledging our employees’ contributions and involvement, by ensuring fair and reasonable returns to our shareholders, and by being responsible, conscientious, trustworthy, and dependable corporate citizens.

Values

Among the standards, morals, ethics, ideals, and tenets of beliefs that we strive to encourage, uphold and sustain with our managerial and service staff, is for us to be relevantly creative but eco-minded, to build lasting rapport and relationships, to always be honest and professional, to extend compassion where required, and to be responsible for our own actions. In addition, we strive to be approachable, are always truthful and sincere, to have dedicated passion for our work and duties, to co-exist as a team and to respect others irrespective of age, race and/or religion, to have courage to embrace change, and to deliver exceptional and exemplary service 24/7 to everyone and anyone who walks through our doors.
